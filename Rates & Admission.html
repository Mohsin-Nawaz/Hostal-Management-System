Rates & Admission

Welcome to Allen Hostel! We hope you already have an idea of what life is like in ACS Oldham Hall through the other pages on our website. While we are positive about the benefits of boarding, we understand not every student is suited for boarding life. If you are keen to board at ACS Oldham Hall, please write to our Marketing Manager, Ms Allen ahmed (allena12@gmail.com) who will assist you with the application process. To visit Allen Hostel, please email or call us to arrange for an appointment.

Criteria

An applicant to ACS Oldham Hall must be: Between 12-18 years of age, but not studying in a tertiary institution or higher

Process

We require the following items for application: A completed Application Form A letter of acceptance from the school (if newly-accepted into the school) Latest academic transcript and discipline record Photocopy of passport An application fee of S$50.00 (non-refundable and to be submitted with application form) Do contact us at enquries@ahmedhostel.org or call us at +95 77752 8443 should you have any queries.

Fee Structure (2018)

One-Time Payment: Application Fee S$50 Miscellaneous and Registration Fee S$320 Deposit(Refundable) S$2,000

Notes :
* Boarding Fee covers lodging, laundry service and meals charged for each school’s semester.
** Applicable to selected schools only
*** Applicable to selected schools only
Explanation: Application Fee A non-refundable application fee is payable upon submission of application form. Registration Fee A non-refundable registration fee is payable once the child has been offered a place in Ahmed hostel. Deposit The deposit is refundable only when the boarder gives one month’s notice in writing and complies with the year-long boarding commitment. Full settlement of boarding fee for the entire semester is to be paid. Any outstanding payment due to ahmed hostel will be deducted from the deposit.
Hospitalisation & Surgical Insurance An annual personal hospitalization and surgical insurance scheme is optional for Singaporean. This coverage will cease upon the withdrawal of the boarder.
Payment Accounts Fees are payable in advance and must be paid within 14 days of receipt of invoice. A penalty will be charged at 2% per month for late payment. The management may refuse permission of stay if fees for any preceding semester have not been paid.
REFUND POLICY:
Deposit will not be refunded to boarder who withdraws without giving sufficient notice. Any remaining boarding fee for the period after the withdrawal date will not be refunded.
